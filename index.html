Tenant Guardrail

Each company gets its own workspace boundary.

Customers, carriers, drivers, shipments, BOLs, and route runs stay inside the signed-in company. Internal WMS 365 admins can switch tenants, but regular company users should never see another company's data.

Workflow Reset

Shipments and routes should be separate records.

First create the shipment, then generate the BOL, then assign the carrier and driver, then publish a route run. That makes status tracking and reporting much easier than the old spreadsheet-linked flow.

Driver App

Routes should end in a mobile inbox, not a print button.

Once a dispatcher assigns a driver, the route should be pushed to a driver mobile app with stops, delivery windows, maps, POD upload, and live status sync.
